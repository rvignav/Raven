About Us

The Raven Ensemble is a nonprofit organization with one mission: play to provide. We play famous classical pieces with a unique, childlike flair in order to raise money to allow students in under-resourced communities to obtain an education and follow their entrepreneurial dreams. We partner with organizations such as Save the Children, Lemonade Day, and Pencils of Promise. Please visit one of our performances to show your support!
